A new level of Authentication for your Website

If you got a web-site, chances are that sooner or later you are going to need an authentication system for your users. KeyGuard will give you the possibility of enabling a continuous authentication system based on keystroke dynamics and in this way you are sure that your users are safe.



Why Keystroke Dynamics?

There are several types of authentication; authenticate by what you have, what you are (or how you behave), what you know etc (more here). The problem with what you have or what you know is that someone else also may know it, or may have it. On the other hand, what you are or how you behave is at most times very unique and very hard to copy (or steal of course). So, studies have been conducted and have shown that a keyboard typing pattern of a person (like hand signature) is very unique, and can be used in a behavioral authentication system.
